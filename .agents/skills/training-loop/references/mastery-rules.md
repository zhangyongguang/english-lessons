# Mastery rules

Track first-attempt accuracy, self-correction, delayed retrieval, and transfer contexts separately.

States: `new`, `learning`, `reviewing`, `mastered`, `relapsed`.

Move to `reviewing` only after at least 10 opportunities, 80% first-attempt accuracy, one successful delayed retest, and two contexts. Move to `mastered` only after a later unprompted pass on another day. Prompted repetition is not a correct first attempt.
